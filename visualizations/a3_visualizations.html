Video Game Visualizations

V1: Global Sales by Genre and Platform

Which genres generate the highest total global sales across all platforms? Are certain platforms especially strong for specific genres?

  • Action, Sports, and Shooter clearly dominate total sales across most platforms.
  • PS2, PS3, Wii, and Xbox platforms show consistently larger bubbles across many genres.
  • Some genres like Strategy and Puzzle are much smaller overall and do not dominate on any platform.

V2: Sales Over Time by Platform and Genre

How did Action and Sports game sales change over time for PS2, Wii, and Xbox 360? Which platforms had the strongest peak performance over time?

  • PS2 shows strong early peaks in both Action and Sports during the early 2000s.
  • Wii displays sharp spikes around its peak generation years.
  • Xbox 360 maintains strong sustained sales during the mid-to-late 2000s.

V3: Regional Sales vs. Platform

Which platforms are strongest in North America compared to Europe and Japan? Do certain consoles show clear regional preference patterns?

  • Xbox platforms perform much stronger in North America compared to Japan.
  • PlayStation platforms are more balanced across NA and EU.
  • Nintendo consoles tend to show stronger relative presence in Japan compared to Xbox.

V4: Special Inquiry

How has the Shooter genre evolved in total sales over time? Which platforms contributed most to the rise of Shooter games?

  • Shooter sales spike heavily during the PS3 and Xbox 360 era.
  • Xbox 360 especially stands out as a major driver of Shooter popularity.
  • Shooter sales decline slightly in later years, suggesting market saturation or genre shift.
